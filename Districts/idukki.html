Idukki

Derived from the Malayalam word 'Idukkui', meaning a narrow gorge Idukki is a slice of heaven. Blessed with one of the most pristine reserve forest, hill stations, tea plantations and wildlife sanctuaries it should definitely find a place in your pit stops in Kerala.

Tourist Places in Idukki

  • amakkalmedu
  • Periyar Tiger Reserve
  • Nedumkandam Hills
  • Peerumedu
  • Thommankuthu falls
  • Idukki Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Kalvari Mount
  • Palkulamedu
  • Munnar
