Welcome to Kerala...The God's own country

Kerala is a state located in the southern part of India, known for its natural beauty, rich culture, and diverse cuisine. It is a popular tourist destination with its palm-lined beaches, serene backwaters, lush greenery, and rolling hills.

Kerala has a long coastline of around 580 km, which boasts of some of the most beautiful beaches in the country, such as Kovalam, Varkala, and Cherai. The state is also known for its network of backwaters, which are interconnected brackish lagoons and lakes that run parallel to the Arabian Sea. Visitors can take a leisurely boat ride through these serene waterways and enjoy the breathtaking views of the lush green landscape.

Apart from its natural beauty, Kerala is also famous for its rich cultural heritage. The state has a long history and a vibrant tradition of dance, music, and art. The classical dance form of Kerala, Kathakali, is world-famous for its elaborate makeup and costumes.
